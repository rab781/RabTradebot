## 2025-04-03 - O(N) optimizations in simulation hot-loops
**Learning:** In intensive loops like Monte Carlo simulations, avoiding repeated `[...trades]` cloning per-iteration prevents massive memory buildup. Fisher-Yates shuffle directly against pre-cloned arrays combined with `Var(X) = E[X^2] - (E[X])^2` algebraic conversions massively drops callback overhead and scales standard deviations effectively.
**Action:** Extract cloning of read-only dataset outside iteration loops. Pre-allocate arrays using `new Array(size)` rather than `[]` + `.push()`, and merge multiple functional method passes (`.reduce`, `.map`) into single mathematically combined `for` loops.
